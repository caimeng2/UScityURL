Codebook for UScityURL.csv

The dataset was collected in September 2022. 
It has five fields: GEOID, MUNICIPALITY, STATE, WEBSITE_AVAILABLE, and WEBSITE_URL. 
- GEOID: A unique identifier for each municipality to connect with geographic and demographic data from the US Census. More information about GEOID is available at https://www.census.gov/programs-surveys/geography/guidance/geo-identifiers.html.
- MUNICIPALITY: Municipality name.
- STATE: State name.
- WEBSITE_AVAILABLE: If an official website is available for a municipality: 1 means available and 0 means not available.
- WEBSITE_URL: The web address of a municipality.
