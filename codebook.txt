Codebook for UScityURL

The dataset was collected in September 2022. 
It has five fields: GISJOIN, CITY, STATE, WEBSITE_AVAILABLE, and WEBSITE_URL. 
- GISJOIN: a unique identifier for each municipality to connect with the spatial files (i.e., shapefiles) and the Census data, which could be obtained from the National Historical Geographic Information System (https://www.nhgis.org/). 
- CITY: city name.
- STATE: state name.
- WEBSITE_AVAILABLE: if an official website is available for a city: 1 means available and 0 means not available.
- WEBSITE_URL: the web address of a city.
